Your Accommodation

Ibo Island Lodge

Quirimbas

Ibo Island Lodge consists of three historic and characterful mansions, each over 100 years old, all of which have been restored to their former glory and offer 14 individually designed rooms. The Lodge is set on a prime waterfront site within walking distance of old historical Ibo town, where it feels like a place forgotten and time has stood still.

Mount Meru Hotel

Ashura + Kilimanjaro

Situated at the foothills of Mount Meru in the city of Arusha, the Mount Meru Hotel lies on 15 acres of serene and lush landscape. The hotel has a gym, a spa, 3 restaurants, 2 pools and the nearby gold course!
