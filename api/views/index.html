Your Itinerary

14 DAYS

  • Itinerary

    Deena and Friends Tanzanian Safari + Beach Vacation

  • Safari Specialist

    Troy Smith

  • Travel Dates

    Sun Jan 20 2019 - Sat Feb 02 2019

  • Adults

    4

  • Status

    Provisional

  • Day 1

    20 JAN

    Upon arrival at Kilimanjaro International Airport (JRO), collect your bags and make your way into the arrivals area where you'll be met by a driver and transferred through to Arusha where you'll overnight. Overnight at Katambuga House in a Deluxe Room.

  • Day 2

    21 JAN

    Meet with your safari guide and depart in your private vehicle. Drive south-west to Tarangire National Park (approx 2.5hrs to the gate). Tarangire National Park is renowned for its huge herds of elephant and beautiful baobab trees. There are also more than 400 species of bird to be found in the park. Overnight at Gibb’s Farm in a Garden Cottage.

  • Day 3

    22 JAN

    Rise early and descend into the ancient Ngorongoro Crater for a full day game drive. Enjoy the stunning views of the ancient, collapsed caldera which is home to a variety of wildlife species. Overnight at Gibb’s Farm in a Garden Cottage.

  • Day 4

    23 JAN

    Fly from Manyara Airstrip (offering excellent views of the Great Rift Valley Escarpment and Lake Manyara) into the Serengeti. Overnight at Olakira Camp in a luxury tent.

    Coastal Aviation flight from Manyara to Ndutu (11:30 - 13:00)

  • Day 5 - 6

    24 JAN - 25 JAN

    Experience game drives in the short-grass plains of the Southern Serengeti which usually plays host to the Great Migration from December to early March each year. In February, the herds 'drop' (ie, give birth) to their young which creates opportunity for the predators in the region!. Overnight at Olakira Camp in a luxury tent.

  • Day 7

    26 JAN

    Fly from the Serengeti through to Selous, changing planes half-way in Ruaha NP. On safari in Selous: experience an array of activities on both land and water. Did you know: Selous is home to one of Africa’s largest populations of African wild dog - one of the continent’s greatest predators?. Overnight in a luxury tent at Roho ya Selous.

    Coastal Aviation flight from Ndutu to Selous (09:30 - 13:05)

  • Day 8 - 9

    27 JAN - 28 JAN

    Experience the wilderness in Selous while on safari at Roho ya Selous - game drives, boating safaris, walking safaris and optional fly camping (available at an extra charge). Overnight in a luxury tent at Roho ya Selous.

  • Day 10

    29 JAN

    Fly from Selous through to Zanzibar (via Dar es Salaam). Upon landing into ZNZ Airport you'll be met and transferred through to your accommodation in Stone Town. Overnight at the Park Hyatt (in the heart of Stone Town) in a Ocean View Suite.

    Coastal Aviation flight from Selous to Zanzibar (09:15 - 11:20)

  • Day 11

    30 JAN

    Transfer from Stone Town through to your beach property. Overnight at Baraza (in an Ocean Front Villa with private plunge pool).

  • Day 12 - 13

    31 JAN - 1 FEB

    Relax by the pool or on the beach, or experience some of the many activities available in Zanzibar - all of which can be organized at the lodge upon arrival. Overnight at Baraza (in an Ocean Front Villa with private plunge pool).

  • Day 14

    2 FEB

    Your final morning by the beach before transferring through to ZNZ Airport for your onward flight to Arusha. Upon landing, you'll be met and transfer through to JRO for your onward international flight.

    Coastal Aviation flight from Zanzibar to Arusha (15:30 - 17:40)
