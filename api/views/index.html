  • Day 1

    11 MAR

    Fly from Johannesburg through to Maun. Upon arrival collect your luggage and pass customs where you'll be met and set off on a charter flight into the Central Kalahari! Upon landing, you'll be met by your guide and meander through to camp. Enjoy afternoon safari activities. Overnight Dinaka in a luxury tent.

    South African Airways flight: Johannesburg - Maun (11:45 - 13:15)

    Charter Flight from Maun to Dinaka airstrip, time to be advised locally, but likely approx 13.45 take-off from Maun.

  • Day 2

    12 MAR

    Timeless and truly isolated, the Central Kalahari is the second largest wildlife reserve in the world and is situated in central Botswana’s Kalahari Desert biome. Endless roads stretch out into limitless wilderness and expansive skies glow with sun-washed blues and intricate cloud formations. The scenery is uniquely appealing, the golden grasslands, bush and fossilized salt pans and river beds, complemented by an overwhelming sense of space. The central Kalahari comes alive after the summer rains, transforming dry landscape to nourishing grasslands capable of supporting massive herds of game. Giraffe, warthog, wildebeest, eland, gemsbok, hartebeest and kudu feast on sweet grasses or succulent trees, and this green season is a natural awakening, with an energy all of its own. Overnight Dinaka in a luxury tent.

  • Day 3

    13 MAR

    Fly from Dinaka to Shinde airstrip in the famed Okavango Delta. Upon landing, you'll be met and set off on a game drive through to Shinde Camp. Overnight at Shinde in your luxury tent.

    Charter Flight from Dinaka airstrip to Shinde airstrip

  • Day 4 - 5

    14 MAR - 15 MAR

    On safari in the Okavango Delta! The seasonal flood from the Angola Highlands is the heartbeat of Botswana. Seeping along the panhandle and into the largest inland delta system in the world, the water creates a uniquely beautiful National Heritage Site, the incoming tide of life bringing with it birth, transformation and replenishment. These ancient, cyclical changes provide an exceptional safari experience and the area supports vast herds of elephant, buffalo and hippo, as well as numerous antelope, giraffe, zebra and the ever present predators. Overnight at Shinde in your luxury tent.

  • Day 6

    16 MAR

    Fly from Shinde airstrip for Kasane airport. Upon arrival you'll be met and transferred through to Victoria Falls Town (around 1hr). Overnight Victoria Falls Hotel in your Deluxe room.

    Charter flight from Shinde airstrip to Kasane Airport (1h20mins)

  • Day 7

    17 MAR

    Discover Victoria Falls Town, and of course the mighty waterfalls themselves! Prepare to get wet if you're travelling during "high water" (April - September), and we'd also suggest visiting the historic Victoria Falls Hotel for High Tea and exploring the local markets. Optional adventure sports and heli-flips available. Overnight Victoria Falls Hotel in your Deluxe room.

  • Day 8

    18 MAR

    Transfer from Victoria Falls River Lodge to Victoria Falls Airport for your flight back to Johannesburg.

    South African Airways flight: Victoria Falls to Johannesburg (13:25 - 15:05)

Your Accommodation

Dinaka

Pans

Dinaka has been completely refurbished since being taken over by Ker and Downey Botswana and is located in an exclusive private reserve hugging the northern edge of the Central Kalahari Game Reserve - a region which hosts one of the most unique bio-diversities in Southern Africa. Dinaka’s tents are well spaced, tastefully styled and raised up on wooden decks giving the perfect vantage point over a permanent water hole. Camp activities include: morning, afternoon and night game drives, educational bush walks with a Bushman, photographic opportunities at an underground bunker and various photographic hides. Maximum 4 guests per vehicle on game drives.

Shinde

Okavango Delta

Shinde is located on a private concession, in the heart of the northern Okavango Delta. Nestled amongst shady trees, on the edge of Shinde Lagoon, this land and water camp commands spectacular views over breath-taking landscape. Spacious and luxuriously appointed, 8 classic safari tents are elevated and extend out to decks with magnificent floodplain or waterway vistas. Cool off in the secluded plunge pool whilst keeping watch for the wild inhabitants. Exciting day and night game drives are conducted in specially adapted open vehicles and guided bush walks are subject to the availability of a walking guide. Enjoy mokoro and boat excursion (subject to water levels), and fishing between March to December.

Victoria Falls Hotel

Victoria Falls

Built in 1904, the historic Victoria Falls Hotel is an icon, its gracious colonial architecture leading out to expansive terraces and lawns overlooking the Victoria Falls Bridge. 161 classic rooms and suites provide modern comfort and the tasteful echoes of the Edwardian past. Décor includes the memorabilia and old-world charm of a bygone era, a nod to the pioneers and explorers whose fame lives on, immortalised in the hotel’s elegant style. Using the mist from the Falls as a beacon, stroll through attractively landscaped gardens along a private pathway, to the scenic Victoria Falls rainforests. High Tea is served on the terrace, a delightful tradition which fits so well with the atmosphere and ethos of this luxurious hotel.
